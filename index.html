About Us

History: Founded and registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission in 2010 (RC 882592), JUSTSTAN NIGERIA LIMITED has established itself as a reputable entity in labor outsourcing. We hold an employer's permit and recruiter’s license from the Federal Ministry of Labour and Employment, covering multiple sectors.

Vision: To be the foremost HR outsourcing partner in Nigeria, delivering excellent value to our clients, fostering growth and excellence in workforce management.

Mission: Our hiring solutions are designed to deliver value-added services, sourcing highly qualified individuals to meet human capital needs on a permanent, temporary, or outsourced basis.
